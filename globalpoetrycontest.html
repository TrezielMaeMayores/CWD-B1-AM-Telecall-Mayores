Enter our poetry contest for a chance to win cash prizes and gain worldwide recognition for your talent!

Guidelines:

  1. Signing up to Poetry.com is required to enter.
  2. Work must be an original composition and previously unpublished.
  3. Your poem should not exceed 75 lines in length including stanza breaks.
  4. Poems may take any poetic form (see Poetic Forms) and cover any theme or subject as long as material is respectful and inoffensive.
  5. There is no limit to the number of poems you may apply with, a new application form must be filled out for each new entry.
  6. Poems must be in English.
  7. We cap total entries at 150 poems per contest.
  8. A non-refundable entry and review fee of 25 USD* is required to enter.

  • priority_highFAQs

  • info_outlineHow does it work?
    Every month, a new contest will begin. Submit an original poem or choose one of your existing poems from the site. Check out our entry criteria, then submit your entry before the deadline! The following month, we will announce the winners on our contest Summary Page. Additionally, all nominated poems will be displayed on our site and will feature a special badge, highlighting it is a contest nominee. Each winner's badge will display their position in the top three!
  • info_outlineHow do you decide who wins?
    In this contest, YOU are the judges! Every nominee is eligible for one vote, each vote worth a single point. Once all the applications have been received or the submission period has been reached, you will be able to vote. You are not allowed to vote for yourself, and abstaining from voting will deduct a point from your poem. On top of that, the Poetry.com team will award five bonus points to their favorite poems. The voting period will last one week, after which the winners will be announced!.
  • info_outlineWait what?
    YES! all our participants are judges too! Our poetry contests boast a clever twist! We thought long and hard about what we can offer the poetry community that is new, fresh and exciting. Traditional formats have a panel of judges, named or anonymous. Our vision is that poetry is like art - it can be viewed and interpreted in a myriad of ways and it all comes down to taste and opinion. We offer a Judgement Guide to outline the key aspects of a good poem and encourage you to bring your own knowledge and experience together with ours when voting for yourselves. We believe in our community and its power to create! Let the games begin!
